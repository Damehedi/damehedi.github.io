About

A builder across commercial, data & operations.

I am a final-year Business Management student who never learned to do just one thing at a time.

Across the last three years I have held up to five concurrent roles spanning luxury hospitality, international B2B sales, live events and NHS patient support, all while completing my degree at London South Bank University on Business Management with Placement.

The common thread is a bias toward fixing what is broken. When I notice a process failing, I do not wait for someone else to raise it. At Inventive I traced a recurring inventory discrepancy through stock analysis and proposed a revised procurement schedule, cutting estimated waste by around 40%. At Accor I analysed recurring guest feedback patterns and proposed service recovery protocols that were adopted across the team.

I bring advanced data skills in Power BI, Excel and SQL, five years of self-directed investing, and a genuine obsession with getting the detail right, whether that detail is a reconciliation, a pitch, or a guest's experience.
