About

A builder across business, data & care.

I am a final-year Business Management student who never learned to do just one thing at a time.

Across the last three years I have held up to five concurrent roles spanning luxury hospitality, NHS administration, international B2B sales and live events, all while completing my degree at London South Bank University on a foundation of Computer Science Engineering.

The common thread is a bias toward building. When I notice a process breaking, I do not wait for someone else to fix it. At Homerton Hospital I spotted that the team had no way to track requests against deadlines, so I built one in Excel. At Accor I noticed colleagues waiting on inbound leads, so I built a data-led prospecting approach that made me Best Seller in my cohort.

I bring advanced data skills in Power BI, Excel and SQL, five years of self-directed investing, and a genuine obsession with getting the detail right, whether that detail is a reconciliation, a pitch, or a guest's experience.
